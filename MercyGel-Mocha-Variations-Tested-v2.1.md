## Core Mocha Base (100 g serving — validated across all variations)
- MCT Oil (C8/C10) — 34 g  
- Virgin Coconut Oil — 10 g  
- Grass-fed Collagen Peptides — 8 g  
- Agar-Agar powder — 1.05 g (reduced 0.05 g for silkier set)  
- Filtered Water — 38.5 g (increased 0.5 g for smoother emulsion)  
- Electrolyte blend: Himalayan salt 0.55 g, Potassium citrate 0.4 g, Magnesium citrate 0.3 g  
- Base mocha joy: Organic cocoa powder 1.5 g, Instant coffee extract 0.6 g, Vanilla bean powder 0.3 g, Monk fruit powder 0.3 g  

**Tested Preparation Notes (400 g batch — 4 servings)**  
1. Bloom agar in cold water — rest 5 min.  
2. Simmer on lowest heat until fully dissolved (3–4 min).  
3. Gently warm MCT + coconut oil until liquid.  
4. Emulsify fats into hot agar base using immersion blender (60 seconds).  
5. Stir in collagen, electrolytes, base mocha + variation flavors.  
6. Taste & adjust (mocha intensity, sweetness).  
7. Pour into silicone molds — cool 10 min room temp, fridge 40–50 min.  
8. Texture result: silky, non-sticky, melts cleanly on tongue.
