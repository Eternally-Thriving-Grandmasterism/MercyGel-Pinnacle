## MercyGel v1.0 — Base Recipe (100 g serving = 1 large pouch)
**Macros (per 100 g)**  
- Calories: \~580 kcal  
- Fat: 50 g (\~78%)  
- Protein: 9 g (\~17%)  
- Net Carbs: ≤3 g  
- Sodium: 450 mg | Potassium: 350 mg | Magnesium: 90 mg  

**Ingredients**  
- 35 g MCT Oil (C8/C10 blend) — fast ketones  
- 10 g Virgin Coconut Oil — rich flavor  
- 8 g Grass-fed Collagen Peptides — clean protein, gut love  
- 1.2 g Agar-Agar — natural vegan gel  
- 40 g Filtered Water — hydration base  
- Electrolytes: 0.6 g Himalayan pink salt, 0.4 g Potassium citrate, 0.3 g Magnesium citrate  
- Valence joy: 0.3 g Vanilla bean powder, 0.2 g Ceylon cinnamon, 0.3 g Monk fruit powder  
- Optional uplift: 0.5 g Lemon zest, 1 g Freeze-dried raspberry powder  

**How to Make (400 g batch — 4 servings)**  
1. Bloom 1.2 g agar-agar in 40 g cold water → wait 5 min.  
2. Simmer gently until dissolved (\~3–5 min).  
3. Warm MCT + coconut oil until liquid.  
4. Whisk fats into agar — emulsify (blender best).  
5. Stir in collagen, electrolytes, vanilla, cinnamon, monk fruit → taste & tweak.  
6. Pour into molds/pouches → cool 10 min → fridge 30–60 min.  
7. Pop out → enjoy!
