## Macros (per 100 g serving)
- Calories: \~570 kcal
- Fat: 50 g (78%)
- Protein: 9 g (17%)
- Net Carbs: ≤3 g
- Electrolytes: Sodium 450 mg, Potassium 350 mg, Magnesium 90 mg

## Ingredients (100 g serving)
1. MCT Oil (C8/C10) — 34 g
2. Virgin Coconut Oil — 10 g
3. Grass-fed Collagen Peptides — 8 g
4. Agar-Agar powder — 1.1 g (optimized for firmer set)
5. Filtered Water — 38 g
6. Electrolyte blend: Himalayan salt 0.55 g, Potassium citrate 0.4 g, Magnesium citrate 0.3 g
7. Mocha valence joy: Organic cocoa powder 1.5 g, Instant coffee extract 0.6 g, Vanilla bean powder 0.3 g, Monk fruit powder 0.3 g
8. Optional boosters: Ceylon cinnamon 0.2 g, Lion’s Mane extract 0.2 g (focus), Ashwagandha 0.1 g (stress)

## Preparation (400 g batch — 4 servings)
1. Bloom agar in cold water — stir, rest 5 min.
2. Simmer agar solution until dissolved (low heat).
3. Gently melt MCT + coconut oil.
4. Emulsify fats into hot agar base (immersion blender).
5. Stir in collagen, electrolytes, cocoa, coffee, vanilla, monk fruit, cinnamon, adaptogens.
6. Taste & adjust mocha intensity.
7. Pour into molds — cool 10 min, fridge 45 min.
8. Store: Fridge 10 days, room temp 3 days sealed.
