## Core Mocha Base (100 g serving — used for all variations)
- MCT Oil (C8/C10) — 34 g  
- Virgin Coconut Oil — 10 g  
- Grass-fed Collagen Peptides — 8 g  
- Agar-Agar powder — 1.1 g  
- Filtered Water — 38 g  
- Electrolyte blend: Himalayan salt 0.55 g, Potassium citrate 0.4 g, Magnesium citrate 0.3 g  
- Base mocha joy: Organic cocoa powder 1.5 g, Instant coffee extract 0.6 g, Vanilla bean powder 0.3 g, Monk fruit powder 0.3 g  

## Preparation (400 g batch — 4 servings) — Common to all variations
1. Bloom agar in cold water — rest 5 min.  
2. Simmer until fully dissolved (low heat).  
3. Gently melt MCT + coconut oil.  
4. Emulsify fats into hot agar base (immersion blender).  
5. Stir in collagen, electrolytes, base mocha components + variation-specific flavors.  
6. Taste & adjust.  
7. Pour into molds — cool 10 min, fridge 45 min.  
8. Store: Fridge 10 days, room temp 3 days sealed.
